Vegetables

Vegetables are organized into 5 subgroups based on their nutrient content.

  1. Dark Green Vegetables
    • Bok Choy
    • Broccoli
    • Collard Greens
  2. Starchy Vegetables
    • Corn
    • White Potatoes
    • Sweet Potatoes
  3. Red and Orange Vegetables
    • Acorn squash
    • Butternut squash
    • Carrots
